Rose Lenehan

I am a postdoctoral fellow in the philosophy department at UCLA. My research is in social & political philosophy. I am especially interested in the political economy of race and the relationship between racism and capitalism. I am also interested in feminist philosophy, Wittgenstein, and Marx.

I spend most of my free time with the LA Tenants Union, a bilingual, member-funded and -run union that fights displacement and evictions.
