Rose Lenehan

Starting in fall 2019 I’ll be a postdoc in the philosophy department at UCLA. My research is in social & political philosophy, especially critical philosophy of race and the political economy of race. My dissertation, “Reparations, Racial Exploitation, and Racial Capitalism,” is a set of papers about the relationship between racial justice and economic justice in the wake of slavery and other forms of racialized extraction.

I am also interested in feminist philosophy, philosophy of language, Wittgenstein, and Marx.
