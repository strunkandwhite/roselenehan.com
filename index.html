Rose Lenehan

I am a postdoctoral fellow in the Center for Humanistic Inquiry and a visiting lecturer in philosophy at Amherst College. Previously, I was a postdoc in the philosophy department at UCLA. My research is in social & political philosophy. I am especially interested in the relationships between race and class and their bearing on questions of racial justice. I am also interested in Marxism, feminist philosophy, and Wittgenstein.

I am involved in tenant organizing and eviction defense work through the LA Tenants Union.
